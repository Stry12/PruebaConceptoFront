# Design System — Hospital Management System

## Visual Identity
Minimalist clinical aesthetic. Clean lines, generous whitespace, clear typographic hierarchy through size and weight (not excessive color). Professional healthcare feel — not playful, not cold corporate.

## Color Palette

### Primary
- Primary: #0F766E (teal medical — trust, cleanliness, clinical association)
- Primary hover: #0D6B63
- Primary light: #CCFBF1
- Primary lighter: #F0FDFA

### Secondary
- Secondary: #2563EB (interactive blue — links, secondary actions)
- Secondary hover: #1D4ED8
- Secondary light: #DBEAFE

### Accent
- Accent: #D97706 (warm amber — warnings, badges, attention without alarm)
- Accent hover: #B45309
- Accent light: #FEF3C7

### Neutrals (blue-gray base)
- 50: #F8FAFC (page background)
- 100: #F1F5F9
- 200: #E2E8F0 (borders, dividers)
- 300: #CBD5E1
- 400: #94A3B8

- 600: #475569 (body text secondary)
- 700: #334155
- 800: #1E293B

### Semantic States
- Success: #16A34A (completed actions)
- Success light: #DCFCE7

### Surfaces
- Background: #F8FAFC

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (body and headings)
- Heading weight: 600 (semibold)
- Body weight: 400 (regular)
- Label weight: 500 (medium)
- Sizes: 12px caption, 14px body, 16px body-md, 18px h3, 22px h2, 28px h1
- Line height: 1.3 headings, 1.5 body

## Spacing
- Base unit: 8px
- Scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64px

## Shape
- Small radius: 4px (inputs, badges)
- Medium radius: 6px (cards, buttons)
- Large radius: 8px (modals, drawers)
- No pill-shaped buttons (max 6px radius)

## Elevation
- Subtle shadows only (2-4% opacity)
- No hard shadows, no gradients

## Iconography
- Outline/stroke icons only, never filled/solid
- Material Symbols weight 300-400

## Layout
- Fixed sidebar: 240px
- Header: 64px height
- Content padding: 32px
- Card padding: 24px
- Form input height: 40px

## Prohibitions
- No filled/solid icons
- No gradients
- No default Bootstrap/Material look
- No pill-shaped buttons
- No neon or oversaturated colors
- No hard shadows
- No placeholders as labels (WCAG AA)
- No long animations (max 200ms transitions)
